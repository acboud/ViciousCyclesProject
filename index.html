Vicious Cycles

Overview

Founded in 2003, Vicious Cycles sells primarily to college students and teens. The shop is especially known for selling racing bikes, mounting bikes, and bmx bikes. Recently has been carrying more and more electric bikes. It has a repair shop and employs mechanics with decades of experience.

Focus is on selling to college students primarily, then teens and children. We sponsor racing teams and events for the community.
